{% elif timePeriod == "today" %}

Daily Occupation

Calculation of the Relative Occupation
The relative occupation is calculated by the number of times the SmartVision Algorithm assessed the workstation to be occupied over the total times of the analysis attempts of the SmartVision Algorithm over the course of the day:

Relative Occupation = (# of occupied readings) / (total # of readings)

The relative availability is calculated by the number of times the SmartVision Algorithm assessed the workstation to be available over the total times of the analysis attempts of the SmartVision Algorithm over the course of the day:

Relative Availability = (# of available readings) / (total # of readings)

Note: total # of readings = # of occupied readings + # of available readings

Daily Dominant Emotions

Calculation of the Dominant Emotions
The score of the emotions is calculated relative (base 10) to the total number of times it was attempted by the SmartVision Algorithm to detect an emotion in the face on an employee over the course of today. Let's demonstrate the calculation of an emotion score using the emotion Happiness as an example:

Time Period: Today
1. Determine the total count the emotion happiness was detected (# of Time Happiness Detected).
2. Determine the total attempts of the SmartVision Algorithm to detect an emotion (# of Emotion Analysis Attempts).
3. Relative emotion score of happiness = ((# of Time Happiness Detected) / (# of Emotion Analysis Attempts)) * 10


This is repeated for every emotion or analysis state visible on the radar chart.
